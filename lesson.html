Introduction to Human Anatomy

32 minutes Dr. Mekkawy

What You'll Learn

In this introductory lesson, we'll cover the fundamental concepts of human anatomy that form the foundation for all medical knowledge. You'll understand the organization of the human body and learn key anatomical terminology.

  • Basic anatomical terminology and body planes
  • Organization levels from cells to organ systems
  • Introduction to body cavities and their contents
  • Overview of the major organ systems

Prerequisites

No prior knowledge required. This course is designed for beginners starting their medical education journey.
